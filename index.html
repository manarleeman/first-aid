👶 First Aid for Kids!

🤕

When Someone Gets Hurt

1. Stay calm and don't panic
2. Tell an adult immediately
3. Don't move the hurt person
4. Be a good helper by staying nearby

🩹

Small Cuts and Scrapes

1. Wash your hands first
2. Clean the cut with water
3. Put on a clean bandage
4. Tell an adult what happened

🆘

Emergency Numbers to Remember

Police: 199
Emergency: 112
Fire Service: 199
Always ask an adult to call!

🏥

When to Get Help Fast

• Someone can't breathe
• Lots of blood
• Person won't wake up
• Very bad pain
Run to get an adult NOW!
